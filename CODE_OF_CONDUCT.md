# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in **Cosmos Vault** a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, education level, gender identity, or background.

## Our Standards
Examples of positive behavior:
- Showing empathy and respect toward others.
- Providing constructive feedback.
- Gracefully accepting and addressing suggestions.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- Using sexualized or discriminatory language.
- Trolling, personal attacks, or insulting comments.
- Publishing private information without consent.

## Our Responsibilities
Maintainers are responsible for clarifying behavior standards and taking fair action when violations occur.

## Scope
Applies to all project spaces and public communications.
